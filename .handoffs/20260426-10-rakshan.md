### 1. Smart transcript chunking (transcript.py)

Added `merge_into_sentences()` to `backend/services/transcript.py`.

**Before**: YouTube json3 gives 1-3 word caption bubbles (2-3s each) — stored raw.
**After**: Consecutive events are merged into natural speech units. A new chunk breaks when:
- Gap to next event > 1.2 seconds (real speaker pause)
- Current text ends with `.?!|` (sentence-final punctuation)
- Current text exceeds 200 characters (safety cap)

Both Telugu AND English captions are merged with the same algorithm. English matching uses nearest-start-time (within 2s) since merging shifts boundaries slightly.

**Impact**: New videos ingested from today onward will have sentence-length segments instead of 2-3 word fragments. Existing videos in the DB are unaffected (re-ingest to get the improvement).
